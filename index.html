Against The Spread

From the executive producer of THE VIRTUOSO

Anthony, a rugged gambler living in a southern trailer park, reignites an old rivalry with Shiloh, a menacing loan shark, in order to fund his nimwitted nephew's ‘get rich quick’ scheme involving a nose-job, a beach house, and an online girlfriend. Things spiral into chaos after Anthony's other nephew, Lorenzo, becomes entangled in a star-crossed romance with Shiloh's daughter, Jamelia, and the three family members are plunged into a web of grave peril.
